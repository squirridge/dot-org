What We're All About

Squirrel Ridge Observatory Centre (SROC) is a Canadian not-for-profit organization founded in 2024. We're on a mission to preserve disappearing knowledge, build stronger communities, and create innovative digital tools that actually make a difference.

Think of us as digital archaeologists meets community builders meets slightly chaotic innovators. We rescue information before it vanishes, create tools for understanding languages, study how cities and nature can better coexist, and incubate wild ideas to improve civic life.

Our Mission

To create open-source tools and resources that preserve cultural knowledge, advance linguistic understanding, promote urban ecology, and foster civic innovation‚Äîall while building communities around shared interests and values.
