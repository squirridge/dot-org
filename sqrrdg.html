Stirring the Squirridge

// SQRRDG: Where off-the-wall ideas meet on-the-ground action

An incubator for bold civic transformation ideas. From smart transit solutions to apolitical advocacy, we're reimagining how communities can evolve. Operating in Barrie and Leslieville, we turn "what if" into "let's do this."

Operating Locations

Two communities, one vision: better civic life through creative solutions

🌊

Barrie Better

"Lake Simcoe's Innovation Shore"

Transforming Barrie from bedroom community to bold civic innovator. We're tackling transit gaps, waterfront activation, and creating connections between old town charm and new tech energy. From GO train frequency campaigns to pop-up innovation labs on Dunlop Street.

Less Evil Leslieville

"Toronto's East End Experimenters"

Making Leslieville less about gentrification, more about genuine community. We're preserving industrial heritage while fostering inclusive innovation. From Queen East streetcar advocacy to converting empty storefronts into community tech hubs and artist spaces.
